Hot Rolled Channel

Steel channels, as in rolled steel C sections, have many uses. They are commonly used in all structural applications where other shapes (I, T, etc.) are used.
It is not uncommon to see C sections used back to back (forming an I shape) with a separation in the middle, creating a structural shape with great flexural strength in both directions. You see this a lot in old highway truss bridges.
